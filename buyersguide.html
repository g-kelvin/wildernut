Our nuts are odorless with a mild sweet taste and buttery but a crunchy texture and free of any contaminants

Macadamia Tetraphylla

Macadamia tetraphylla is a tree in the family Proteaceae, native to southern Queensland and northern New South Wales in Australia. Common names include macadamia nut, bauple nut, prickly macadamia, Queensland nut, rough-shelled bush nut and rough-shelled Queensland nut. Macadamia nuts have higher levels of monounsaturated fatty acids (MUFA) than any other food source known.
